Core Services

Global Logistics Solutions

End-to-end freight solutions between China and Australia, covering sea freight, air freight, and customs clearance. Whether you're importing or exporting, we manage the full journey.

01

Global Logistics (Ocean Freight)

End-to-end freight solutions between China and Australia, covering sea freight for containerised and consolidated shipments. Direct shipping from all major Chinese ports to Melbourne.

  • FCL & LCL shipping
  • Door-to-door delivery
  • Reliable scheduling
  • Ports: Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Ningbo, Shanghai, Qingdao, Tianjin, Dalian
02

Fast and Priority Air Freight

Fast and reliable air freight services for urgent and high-value shipments across major cities. Specialist in technology equipment, garments, and electronic products from China.

  • Express and priority shipping
  • Real-time cargo tracking
  • Secure handling for sensitive goods
  • Flexible delivery solutions
03

Customs Clearance

Professional customs clearance services to ensure smooth and timely delivery. Our Melbourne team handles all import & export documentation for commercial and personal goods.

  • Import & export clearance
  • Quarantine inspection support
  • Documentation handling
  • ChAFTA certificate assistance
